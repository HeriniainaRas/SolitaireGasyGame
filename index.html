Solitaire Gasy

The Solitaire is, as its name suggests, a game where you have no opponent. He plays alone. The goal is to move balls on a tray, remove them little by little according to a precise rule until it only remains a single ball on the tray. There are two main types of Solitaire:
The Malagasy Solitaire(37 balls) and the English Solitaire(33 balls).
